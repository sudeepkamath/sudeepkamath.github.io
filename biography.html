Sudeep Kamath – Biography

  • 2014-present: Center for Science of Information postdoc at Princeton University & Purdue University

  • 2013-2014: ITA postdoc at UC San Diego

  • 2008-2013: Graduate student at the University of California, Berkeley

  • 2004-2008: Undergraduate student at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay

Brief Biography:

Sudeep Kamath is a postdoctoral scholar at Princeton University. He received the Bachelor of Technology degree in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2008. He received the M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in 2011 and the M.A. degree in Statistics, and Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in 2013, all from the University of California, Berkeley. He was a postdoctoral scholar at the University of California, San Diego from 2013-14. He is a recipient of the Eliahu Jury Award from the EECS Department of UC Berkeley (2013) and the Institute Silver Medal from the EE Department at IIT Bombay (2008).
